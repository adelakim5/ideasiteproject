About

This site is created for the assignment of likelion. The Contents and Ideas are powered by Yangwon Kim, who is currently studying at Chungnam National University in Daejeon, South Korea. This website is made for all people those who wanna make a quick reservation within 5-minuite. In Gungdong neardy CNU, there are so many people-including me^^- wanna enter into a restaurant and have their meals in a short time especially during the lunch time. However, it is seldom easy to find a proper spot where I don't need to stand in a waiting line and immediately order my menu.
So, I think about a Quick Reservation Service Program which send a message to restaurant manager that "I will arrive within just 5 minitues! Please hold a table!" With this service, we can save the time to find a proper restaurtant, to wait for entering, and even avoid a lot of stressful situations! Honestly it was created for the assignment of Likelion, but I still desire to implement and provide it to lots of people for their better campus-lunch-life!
